Some random commands that may prove useful.  Anything I find interesting
will, ideally, be added here.

+------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| Command          | Description                                         |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
:edit <filename>   | Jumps to <filename>                                 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
:vsplit <filename> | Splits screen vertically and opens <filename>
                   |
    CTRL+w, l      | Switches to the right split window
    CTRL+w, h      | Switches to the left split window
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
